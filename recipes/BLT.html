BLT

Description

Bacon, lettuce and tomato in a white bread sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 5g salted butter, spreadable
  • 2 rashers of unsmoked bacon
  • 4 slices of fresh tomato
  • fresh lettuce leaf

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon until crispy
  2. Spread the butter on one side of each piece of bread
  3. Place fresh lettuce on top of one slice
  4. Place cooked bacon on top of the lettuce
  5. Place sliced tomato on top of the bacon
  6. Top with the second slice of bread
